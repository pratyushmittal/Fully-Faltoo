ToRead - An email-based “Read-It-Later” service

How often you stumble upon an interesting article and due to paucity of time unable to read it ? For me it happens quite a few times and till now I ended up just adding it to bookmarks.

ToRead is an awesome service and mails the complete article (along with source link) to your inbox.

Give it a try!!!
