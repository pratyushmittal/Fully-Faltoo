Sri Lankan team attacked

Posted on Wed 04 March 2009

They came on rickshaws. They were armed with rockets, hand grenades, kalashnikovs.

—HABIB-UR REHMAN,

chief of police in Lahore, Pakistan, after an armed attack on the Sri Lankan cricket team’s bus left several players wounded and five policeman dead.
