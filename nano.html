Ten Things You Should Know About the Nano
Does it have enough power to pass a truck on the highway?

Maybe a small truck. The Nano can do 0 to 100 km in 23 seconds. For most regular city driving, the two-cylinder engine would be fine, but it loses some of its power when the AC is running. (via Ten Things You Should Know About the Nano - Photo Essays - TIME)
